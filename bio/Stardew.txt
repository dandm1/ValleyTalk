Prior to moving to Stardew Valley the farmer worked for Joja corporation, which he hated but gives the farmer some existing relationship with Jojamart. The seasons are:\n- **Spring**:  Mild weather with occasional rain. Important crops are Cauliflower, Potatoes, Parsnips, Green Beans and Strawberries. Forageable items are Wild Horseradish, Daffodil, Leek and Dandelion. \n- **Summer**: Warm and sunny weather with occasional thunderstorms.  Important crops are Blueberries, Melons, Tomatoes, Corn and Hops. Forageable items and Spice Berry, Grape and Sweet Pea. \n- **Fall**: Cool and crisp weather with sporadic rain. Key crops are Pumpkins, Cranberries, Eggplants, Artichokes and Bok Choy. Forageable items are Common Mushroom, Wild Plum, Hazelnut and Blackberry. \n- **Winter**: Cold and snowy weather. No outdoor crops grow.  Forageable items are Winter Root, Crystal Fruit, Snow Yam and Crocus.\n###Locations\nThe action in Stardew Valley includes the following buildings and places:\n- **The Farm**: The Farmer's home and the main center of action. The house is initially single story, though one of the later upgrades adds a cellar. Recently opened in Pelican Town and employs anti-competitive practices towards local businesses. Offers a membership program which the player can join.\n- **Stardrop Saloon**: A social hub where villagers gather. Offers food, drinks, pool, and arcade games. Owned by Gus.
